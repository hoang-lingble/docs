## Product Listing Page (PLP)

### Overview

The PLP enables customers to browser products within a selected category, apply filters, and navigate efficiently to product detail pages.

### Page Layout

- The Grid Layout
  - 3-4 columns (desktop)
  - 1-2 columns (mobile)
- Consistent product card sizing
- Pagination options: 30 / 45 / 60 items
- Breadcrum avigation: Home \> Category \> Sub-category

### Product Cards

- Product thumbnail
- Product title
- Price
- Hover image (alternate view)
- Product badge
- Color swatches
- Availability tag
- Entire card or image is clickable

### Filtering & Sorting

- Filtering
  - Category
  - Size
- Sorting
  - Newest first
  - Best Sellers
  - Price
  - Alphabetical

### Wishlist

- Heart icon available on each product card.
- Clicking adds product to wishlist.
